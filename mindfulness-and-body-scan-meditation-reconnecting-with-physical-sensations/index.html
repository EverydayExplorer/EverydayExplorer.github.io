Mindfulness and Body Scan Meditation: Reconnecting with Physical Sensations

4 min · 813 words

Have you ever found yourself getting lost in a whirlwind of thoughts and emotions, without really being present in the moment? Oftentimes, modern day living can be extremely overwhelming, leaving us feeling disconnected from our bodies and the present moment. In such cases, practicing mindfulness and body scan meditation can work wonders in helping us reconnect with our physical sensations and attain a sense of inner peace and calm.

What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness, in its essence, refers to the practice of being present in the moment, with a non-judgmental and accepting attitude towards oneself and one’s surroundings. Mindfulness meditation is a technique that helps cultivate awareness of one’s thoughts, feelings, and bodily sensations, with the aim of attaining a greater understanding and acceptance of oneself.

What is a Body Scan Meditation?

Body scan meditation is a type of mindfulness meditation that involves bringing attention and awareness to different parts of our body, one body part at a time, moving gradually from the toes up to the head. The practice involves mental noting of any sensations that arise in the different body parts, without any attempt to change or control them.

How can Body Scan Meditation help us Reconnect with our Physical Sensations?

Oftentimes, in our daily lives, we tend to overlook our physical sensations as we get lost in the busyness of our minds. This can lead to feelings of disconnection from our bodies, and eventually, a sense of dis-ease. Practicing body scan meditation can help us bring attention to our bodily sensations, helping us reconnect with our physical selves, and ultimately, attain a sense of inner peace and calm.

Here are some ways that body scan meditation can help you reconnect with your physical sensations:

1. Alleviates Stress and Anxiety

In the current scenario of pandemic, stress and anxiety have become like second nature to people. Body Scan Meditation involves deep breathing and focusing on the bodily sensations. When we focus on our breath and our bodily sensations, we create a sense of calm within ourselves. Body scan meditation has been found to be extremely effective in reducing stress and anxiety levels.

2. Enhances Mind-Body Connection

Body scan meditation involves bringing attention and focus to different parts of our body. This process helps us become more aware of our body and the sensations it is experiencing. Such awareness, over time, strengthens our mind-body connection. This heightened awareness can also help prevent injury or illness resulting from neglect or overuse of our bodies.

3. Increases Self-Awareness

Body scan meditation helps bring attention to our bodily sensations, which in turn, helps increase our self-awareness. When we become aware of our thoughts, feelings, and bodily sensations, we are better able to understand and accept ourselves. Body scan meditation can unearth hidden emotions, beliefs, and patterns of behavior, allowing you to become more self-aware.

4. Improves Sleep Quality

The deep relaxation that results from body scan meditation can help improve the quality of our sleep. It helps calm our mind and relax our muscles, allowing us to fall into a restful state more easily and quickly.

In conclusion, body scan meditation is an effective technique for reconnecting with our physical sensations. This practice helps alleviate stress and anxiety, enhances mind-body connection, increases self-awareness, and improves sleep quality. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced meditator, body scan meditation is an accessible and simple practice that can help you reconnect with your physical sensations, and ultimately, find a sense of inner peace and calm.
