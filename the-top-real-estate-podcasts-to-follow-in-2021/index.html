The Top Real Estate Podcasts to Follow in 2021

3 min · 566 words

Are you a real estate enthusiast looking for the best podcasts to stay updated with industry trends in 2021? Look no further! In this comprehensive guide, we have compiled a list of the top real estate podcasts that you should follow this year. Whether you are a seasoned investor, a first-time homebuyer, or simply curious about the market, these podcasts offer valuable insights, tips, and expert advice to help you navigate the dynamic world of real estate.

  1. The Real Estate Guys Radio Show: Hosted by Robert Helms and Russell Gray, this podcast covers a wide range of real estate topics, including market analysis, investment strategies, and interviews with industry experts. With over 25 years of experience, the hosts provide valuable insights for both beginners and experienced investors.

  2. BiggerPockets Podcast: BiggerPockets is a well-known name in the real estate community, and their podcast is no exception. Hosted by Brandon Turner and David Greene, this show features interviews with successful real estate investors, authors, and entrepreneurs. From rental property investing to flipping houses, this podcast covers it all.

  3. The Real Wealth Show: Hosted by Kathy Fettke, this podcast focuses on wealth-building through real estate investing. With a focus on passive income and financial freedom, the show provides practical tips and strategies for investors looking to grow their portfolios.

  4. Investing in Real Estate with Clayton Morris: Clayton Morris, a former Fox News anchor turned real estate investor, shares his insights on building passive income through rental properties. The podcast covers everything from finding deals to managing properties effectively.

  5. The Commercial Real Estate Show: If you are interested in commercial real estate, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Michael Bull, the show features interviews with industry experts, market updates, and tips for navigating the commercial real estate landscape.

  6. The Millionaire Real Estate Investor Podcast: Hosted by Jay Papasan, this podcast is based on the bestselling book ‘The Millionaire Real Estate Investor’ by Gary Keller. The show offers practical advice for aspiring investors looking to achieve financial success through real estate.

  7. Real Estate Investing Mastery: Hosted by Joe McCall, this podcast is dedicated to helping investors generate leads, close deals, and build a successful real estate business. Whether you are interested in wholesaling, lease options, or creative financing, this show has something for you.

  8. The Property Couch: Hosted by Bryce Holdaway and Ben Kingsley, this Australian-based podcast covers all things real estate, from property investment strategies to market trends. The hosts provide valuable insights for investors in the Australian market.

  9. The Real Estate Syndication Show: Hosted by Whitney Sewell, this podcast focuses on real estate syndication and passive investments. If you are interested in raising capital, forming partnerships, and investing in larger projects, this show is a valuable resource.

  10. Real Estate Rockstars: Hosted by Pat Hiban, this podcast features interviews with top-performing real estate agents and brokers. Whether you are an agent looking to boost your sales or a homeowner seeking tips for selling your property, this show offers valuable advice from industry professionals.

In conclusion, these top real estate podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge and expertise for anyone looking to succeed in the dynamic world of real estate. By following these shows, you can stay informed, motivated, and inspired to achieve your real estate goals in 2021 and beyond. Happy listening!
