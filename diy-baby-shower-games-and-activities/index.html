DIY Baby Shower Games and Activities

3 min · 441 words

Planning a baby shower is an exciting and joyful experience. It’s a chance to celebrate the impending arrival of a little bundle of joy and shower the expectant parents with love and support. One of the key elements of a successful baby shower is the games and activities that bring everyone together and create lasting memories. If you’re looking to add a personal touch to your baby shower, why not try some fun and unique DIY baby shower games and activities? In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of ideas that will make your baby shower an unforgettable event.

1. Diaper Derby:
Turn the traditional diaper-changing task into a friendly competition with the Diaper Derby game. Divide the guests into teams and provide each team with a doll, a diaper, and a set of baby clothes. Set a timer and see which team can change the diaper and dress the doll the fastest. This game is not only entertaining but also a great way to practice those diaper-changing skills!

2. Baby Food Taste Test:
Put your taste buds to the test with the Baby Food Taste Test game. Remove the labels from various jars of baby food and number them. Have guests take turns blindfolded, tasting each jar and guessing the flavor. The guest with the most correct answers wins a prize. This game is sure to bring some laughs and surprises!

3. Onesie Decorating:
Get creative with onesie decorating! Provide plain white onesies, fabric markers, and iron-on patches or stencils. Let your guests unleash their artistic talents and decorate onesies for the baby. Not only will this activity result in adorable custom onesies, but it will also serve as a cherished keepsake for the parents-to-be.

4. Guess the Baby Picture:
Collect baby pictures of the expectant parents and their close family members. Display the pictures on a board or create a slideshow. Have guests try to match each baby picture with the correct adult. This game is a wonderful way to reminisce and learn more about the parents’ childhoods.

5. Baby Name Game:
Challenge your guests’ knowledge of baby names with the Baby Name Game. Create a list of popular baby names and their meanings. Have guests match the names with their corresponding meanings. The guest with the most correct answers wins a prize. This game is not only entertaining but also educational!

6. Diaper Raffle:
Encourage your guests to bring a pack of diapers by hosting a Diaper Raffle. Each guest who brings a pack of diapers gets a raffle ticket. At the end of the baby shower, draw a ticket and award a prize to the lucky winner. This game is a win-win, as the parents-to-be receive a stockpile of diapers while guests have a chance to win a prize.

7. Baby Bottle Chug:
Put your drinking skills to the test with the Baby Bottle Chug game. Fill baby bottles with a non-alcoholic beverage. Have guests compete to see who can drink the contents of the bottle the fastest. This game is sure to generate some friendly competition and laughter.

These DIY baby shower games and activities are just a few ideas to make your baby shower a memorable event. Remember to personalize them to suit the parents’ tastes and preferences. With a little creativity and planning, you can create an unforgettable baby shower experience that will be cherished for years to come. So get ready to have some fun and celebrate the upcoming arrival of a precious little one!
