10 Inspirational Songs for Motivation and Positivity

4 min · 653 words

As humans, we often turn to music to boost our mood and find motivation. Inspirational songs can help us get through difficult times and push us towards being our best selves. Whether we’re facing challenges in our personal or professional lives, motivational music can provide the encouragement and inspiration we need to keep going.

In this post, we’ll be looking at ten inspirational songs that promote positivity, motivation, and resilience. These songs are sure to lift your spirits and give you the push you need to keep working towards your goals.

1. ‘Eye of the Tiger’ by Survivor

This classic motivational song is a popular choice for athletes and anyone who wants to feel like a champion. ‘Eye of the Tiger’ is all about pushing yourself to be your best and never giving up, no matter what obstacles come your way.

2. ‘Roar’ by Katy Perry

‘Roar’ is a powerful anthem about finding your voice and standing up for yourself. This upbeat pop song encourages you to be bold, confident, and unapologetic in pursuing your dreams.

3. ‘Shake It Off’ by Taylor Swift

‘Shake It Off’ is a catchy pop song that reminds us to brush off criticism and negativity. The song encourages listeners to focus on their own happiness and let go of things that are holding them back.

4. ‘Survivor’ by Destiny’s Child

‘Survivor’ is an uplifting song about overcoming adversity and coming out stronger on the other side. The song reminds us that we can get through anything with determination and resilience.

5. ‘Stronger’ by Kelly Clarkson

‘Stronger’ is an empowering song that encourages listeners to pick themselves up and keep going after setbacks. The song is all about finding strength and resilience in difficult times.

6. ‘I Will Survive’ by Gloria Gaynor

‘I Will Survive’ is a disco classic that has become a timeless anthem of resilience. The song encourages us to keep going in the face of adversity and to never give up on ourselves.

7. ‘Don’t Stop Believin’’ by Journey

‘Don’t Stop Believin’’ is a classic rock song that has become a staple of motivational playlists everywhere. The song encourages us to keep dreaming and to never give up on our goals, no matter how big they may seem.

8. ‘Hall of Fame’ by The Script ft. will.i.am

‘Hall of Fame’ is an uplifting song about achieving greatness and leaving a legacy. The song encourages us to believe in ourselves and to work hard towards our goals, no matter what anyone else says.

9. ‘Rise Up’ by Andra Day

‘Rise Up’ is a soulful ballad about finding strength in difficult times. The song encourages us to keep pushing through adversity and to believe in ourselves even when things feel impossible.

10. ‘It’s My Life’ by Bon Jovi

‘It’s My Life’ is a rock anthem about living life on your own terms. The song encourages us to be true to ourselves and to take risks in pursuing our dreams.

In conclusion, music can be a powerful tool for motivation and positivity. Whether we’re facing challenges in our personal or professional lives, inspirational songs can provide the encouragement and inspiration we need to keep going. These ten songs are just a few examples of the many powerful anthems that can help us find strength, resilience, and determination. So next time you need a little extra motivation, turn up the volume and let the music inspire you!
