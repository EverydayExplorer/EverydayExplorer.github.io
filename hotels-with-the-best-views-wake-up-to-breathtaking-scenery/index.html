Hotels with the Best Views: Wake Up to Breathtaking Scenery

4 min · 642 words

Imagine waking up to a picturesque view of turquoise waters stretching as far as the eye can see, or being greeted by the sight of majestic snow-capped mountains bathed in the hues of a rising sun. For avid travelers and nature enthusiasts alike, staying at a hotel with breathtaking views is an experience that transcends ordinary accommodation. In this blog post, we will take you on a virtual journey to some of the world’s most awe-inspiring hotels, where every morning brings an unforgettable vista.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA
Nestled amidst the dramatic desert landscape of Canyon Point, Utah, Amangiri offers a stunning blend of luxury and natural beauty. Its minimalist architecture seamlessly merges with the surrounding red rock formations, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the awe-inspiring vistas. Wake up to panoramic views of the Grand Staircase-Escalante National Monument, and as the sun sets, witness the colors of the desert come alive in a breathtaking display.

2. Jade Mountain Resort, St. Lucia
Perched high above the sparkling waters of the Caribbean Sea, Jade Mountain Resort in St. Lucia offers a truly unique experience. Each of its luxurious suites, known as ‘sanctuaries,’ features an open-air design, allowing uninterrupted views of the Pitons, the iconic volcanic peaks that rise majestically from the azure waters. Imagine sipping your morning coffee while gazing at the lush tropical landscape and the vibrant coral reefs below.

3. Matakauri Lodge, Queenstown, New Zealand
Nestled on the shores of Lake Wakatipu, with the backdrop of the majestic Remarkables mountain range, Matakauri Lodge in Queenstown, New Zealand, is a haven for nature lovers. The lodge’s floor-to-ceiling windows offer breathtaking views of the snow-capped peaks and the crystal-clear lake. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely breakfast or indulging in a sunset cocktail, the scenery will leave you in awe.

4. Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort, Thailand
Escape to the lush jungles of Northern Thailand and experience the magic of Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort. Perched on a ridge overlooking the convergence of the Mekong and Ruak Rivers, the resort offers panoramic views of the mist-covered mountains of Laos and Myanmar. Wake up to the sight of elephants roaming freely in the distance, as you take in the tranquility of this enchanting landscape.

5. The Oberoi Amarvilas, Agra, India
For those seeking a view that is steeped in history and romance, The Oberoi Amarvilas in Agra, India, is the perfect choice. Located just 600 meters from the iconic Taj Mahal, this luxurious hotel offers uninterrupted views of this architectural masterpiece. Imagine waking up to the sight of the Taj Mahal bathed in the soft morning light, an experience that will leave you breathless.

These are just a few examples of the many hotels around the world that offer unforgettable views. Whether you prefer the serenity of mountains, the tranquility of the sea, or the majesty of historical landmarks, there is a hotel out there that will fulfill your every desire. So, why settle for ordinary when you can wake up to breathtaking scenery?

Indulge your senses, embark on a journey of discovery, and let these hotels with the best views transport you to a world where every morning brings a new adventure.
