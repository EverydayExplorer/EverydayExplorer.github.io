The Art of Speedrunning: Tips and Tricks for Completing Games Fast

4 min · 648 words

Speedrunning has become a beloved pastime for gamers worldwide, offering a unique challenge and an opportunity to showcase mastery of game mechanics. Whether you’re aiming to set world records or simply looking to complete your favorite games as quickly as possible, speedrunning requires skill, strategy, and a deep understanding of game mechanics. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore the art of speedrunning and provide valuable tips and tricks for completing games fast.

  1. Choose Your Game Wisely: Not all games are created equal when it comes to speedrunning. Some games feature complex mechanics, lengthy cutscenes, or random elements that make speedrunning difficult or impractical. Start by choosing a game that is well-suited for speedrunning, with clear objectives, consistent mechanics, and a dedicated speedrunning community.

  2. Study the Game: Before diving into a speedrun, take the time to thoroughly study the game you’ll be running. Familiarize yourself with the game’s mechanics, levels, shortcuts, and glitches. Watch speedruns from top players to learn advanced techniques and strategies. The more you know about the game, the better prepared you’ll be to tackle a speedrun.

  3. Practice, Practice, Practice: Like any skill, speedrunning requires practice to master. Set aside dedicated time for practice sessions, focusing on different segments of the game and refining your techniques. Experiment with different routes, strategies, and optimizations to find the fastest path to victory. Don’t get discouraged by setbacks—perseverance is key to improving as a speedrunner.

  4. Master Movement and Execution: Movement is crucial in speedrunning, as even small optimizations can shave seconds off your time. Practice precise movement techniques such as bunny hopping, strafe jumping, and wall jumping to navigate levels quickly and efficiently. Focus on mastering execution to minimize mistakes and maximize speed.

  5. Learn Glitches and Exploits: Many speedruns rely on glitches, exploits, and sequence breaks to complete games faster than intended. Take the time to learn these tricks and techniques, as they can dramatically reduce your completion time. Experiment with different glitches and exploits to find ones that are reliable and consistent.

  6. Optimize Your Route: A well-planned route is essential for a successful speedrun. Analyze each level or area of the game to identify the fastest path to the finish line. Look for shortcuts, skips, and optimizations that can save time and avoid unnecessary detours. Continuously refine your route based on new discoveries and optimizations.

  7. Stay Calm Under Pressure: Speedrunning can be stressful, especially during record attempts or live events. Stay calm under pressure and focus on executing your run to the best of your ability. Don’t let mistakes or setbacks derail your momentum—stay focused and determined to achieve your goal.

  8. Join the Community: The speedrunning community is a valuable resource for aspiring speedrunners. Join online forums, Discord servers, and social media groups to connect with fellow speedrunners, share strategies and tips, and participate in events and competitions. The community can provide support, encouragement, and valuable feedback to help you improve as a speedrunner.

  9. Celebrate Your Achievements: Speedrunning is a challenging and rewarding pursuit, and every improvement and personal best is worth celebrating. Whether you’re shaving seconds off your time or setting new world records, take pride in your achievements and enjoy the journey of mastering your favorite games.

In conclusion, speedrunning is a thrilling and challenging pursuit that requires skill, strategy, and dedication. By choosing the right game, studying its mechanics, practicing diligently, mastering movement and execution, learning glitches and exploits, optimizing your route, staying calm under pressure, joining the community, and celebrating your achievements, you can become a proficient speedrunner and take on the challenge of completing games fast. Whether you’re aiming for world records or simply looking to challenge yourself, speedrunning offers endless opportunities for excitement, creativity, and skill development.
