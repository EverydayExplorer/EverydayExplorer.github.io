The Top 10 Whiskey Brands to Try in 2021

4 min · 642 words

There’s no denying that whiskey is a beloved spirit enjoyed by connoisseurs and casual drinkers alike. From its rich history to the diverse range of flavors, whiskey has captivated the hearts of many. With 2021 well underway, it’s the perfect time to explore the top whiskey brands that are making waves this year. Whether you’re a seasoned whiskey aficionado or a curious novice, this list will have something for everyone.

1. The Macallan

The Macallan is a name that needs no introduction in the world of whiskey. Known for its exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail, The Macallan has been producing some of the finest single malt Scotch whiskies for centuries. From the rich and smooth flavors of the Sherry Oak range to the complex and nuanced expressions of the Fine Oak collection, The Macallan offers an unparalleled drinking experience.

2. Glenfiddich

Glenfiddich is another well-established brand that continues to impress whiskey enthusiasts year after year. It is one of the few distilleries in Scotland that is still family-owned and operated. With a dedication to traditional methods and time-honored recipes, Glenfiddich consistently produces whiskies that are both classic and innovative. Whether you prefer the smooth and honeyed notes of their 12 Year Old expression or the robust and peaty flavors of their Experimental Series, Glenfiddich has something for everyone.

3. Jameson

If you’re looking for an Irish whiskey that blends tradition with a modern twist, look no further than Jameson. With a history dating back to 1780, Jameson has perfected the art of triple distillation, resulting in a whiskey that is smooth, approachable, and incredibly versatile. Whether you enjoy it neat, on the rocks, or in a classic whiskey cocktail, Jameson’s distinctive flavor profile makes it a staple in any whiskey lover’s collection.

4. Balvenie

Balvenie is a distillery that takes pride in its tradition of handcrafted whiskey-making. Located in the heart of Speyside, Scotland, Balvenie produces whiskies that are known for their smoothness, complexity, and depth of flavor. From the silky texture of their 12 Year Old DoubleWood to the sweet and spicy notes of their Caribbean Cask, each bottling from Balvenie is a testament to the skill and expertise of their master distillers.

5. Bulleit

For those who prefer a bold and robust bourbon, Bulleit is the brand to try. With a high rye content and a distinctive spicy kick, Bulleit has gained a loyal following among whiskey enthusiasts. Their flagship Bulleit Bourbon boasts flavors of vanilla, honey, and a touch of oak, making it a perfect sipping whiskey. Additionally, Bulleit Rye offers a bolder and more assertive flavor profile, with notes of peppery spice and dried fruit.

6. Yamazaki

Whiskey lovers seeking a taste of the East should look no further than Yamazaki. Hailing from Japan, Yamazaki has quickly gained recognition as one of the top whiskey brands worldwide. With a meticulous attention to detail and a focus on craftsmanship, Yamazaki produces whiskies that beautifully combine traditional Scottish methods with uniquely Japanese influences. From their flagship Yamazaki 12 Year Old to their limited edition single malts, Yamazaki offers a taste of Japan worth savoring.

7. Woodford Reserve

Woodford Reserve is a brand that embodies the rich heritage and tradition of Kentucky bourbon. Set in the heart of bourbon country, Woodford Reserve is known for its commitment to the ‘Five Sources of Flavor’ – grain, water, fermentation, distillation, and maturation. This attention to detail results in a bourbon that is balanced, smooth, and bursting with flavor. Whether you’re sipping it neat or enjoying it in a classic Old Fashioned, Woodford Reserve is a must-try for any bourbon enthusiast.

8. Talisker

For those who enjoy a peaty and smoky whiskey, Talisker is an excellent choice. Hailing from the Isle of Skye in Scotland, Talisker’s whiskies are known for their maritime character and distinct smoky flavor. Ranging from the smooth and lively expressions of their 10 Year Old to the more intense and complex offerings of their Distillers Edition, Talisker’s whiskies transport you to the rugged and windswept coastline of Scotland with every sip.

9. High West

High West is a unique whiskey brand that combines the traditions of the Old West with the innovation of modern distilling techniques. Located in Utah, High West sources and blends whiskies from around the world to create their distinctive expressions. With a focus on small-batch production and a commitment to quality, High West offers whiskies that are as intriguing as they are delicious. From their smooth and approachable American Prairie Bourbon to their rich and luxurious Rendezvous Rye, High West has something for every whiskey lover.

10. Buffalo Trace

Last but certainly not least, Buffalo Trace is a brand that consistently delivers exceptional whiskies. Situated in Kentucky, the heartland of bourbon, Buffalo Trace has a long-standing reputation for producing some of the finest bourbons in the world. With a wide range of expressions, from the classic Buffalo Trace Bourbon to the more experimental and limited edition releases, Buffalo Trace offers something for every palate. Each whiskey is made with care and attention to detail, resulting in rich, flavorful, and well-balanced spirits.

In conclusion, the whiskey landscape is vast and diverse, offering a multitude of flavors and styles to suit every preference. Whether you’re a fan of Scotch, bourbon, or Irish whiskey, there are countless options to explore and enjoy in 2021. From the classic and timeless offerings of brands like The Macallan and Glenfiddich to the bold and innovative releases of distilleries like Bulleit and Yamazaki, there is no shortage of top-notch whiskies to try this year. So, raise a glass and embark on a whiskey journey that will tantalize your taste buds and deepen your appreciation for this beloved spirit. Cheers!
