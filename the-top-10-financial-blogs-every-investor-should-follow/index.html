The Top 10 Financial Blogs Every Investor Should Follow

3 min · 569 words

In today’s fast-paced world, staying informed about the latest financial news and trends is crucial for investors. With countless financial blogs available online, it can be challenging to determine which ones are worth your time. To help you navigate this vast landscape, we have compiled a list of the top 10 financial blogs that every investor should follow. These blogs offer valuable insights, expert analysis, and actionable advice that can help you make informed investment decisions.

1. Nerd’s Eye View
Written by Michael Kitces, a renowned financial planner and industry thought leader, Nerd’s Eye View is a must-read blog for financial professionals and investors alike. Kitces covers a wide range of topics, including investment strategies, financial planning, and industry trends, all with a focus on evidence-based practices.

2. The Big Picture
Barry Ritholtz, the founder of Ritholtz Wealth Management, is the mastermind behind The Big Picture. This blog offers in-depth analysis of market trends, economic data, and geopolitical events, with a focus on providing context and historical perspective. Ritholtz’s unique insights and engaging writing style make this blog a favorite among investors.

3. A Wealth of Common Sense
Written by Ben Carlson, a former institutional portfolio manager, A Wealth of Common Sense offers practical advice and insights for individual investors. Carlson’s blog covers topics such as behavioral finance, investment strategies, and market history, all with a focus on helping readers build long-term wealth.

4. The Irrelevant Investor
The Irrelevant Investor, authored by Ben Felix, provides in-depth analysis of investment topics, including asset allocation, portfolio construction, and risk management. Felix’s blog is designed to help investors make better decisions by focusing on the most relevant aspects of investing and avoiding common pitfalls.

5. Tim Ferriss Show
Although best known for his best-selling books, Tim Ferriss also maintains a popular blog that features interviews with top investors, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders. The Tim Ferriss Show offers valuable insights into the minds of some of the most successful people in the world, providing inspiration and practical advice for investors.

6. Abnormal Returns
Written by Tadas Viskanta, Abnormal Returns is a daily compilation of the best financial articles and insights from around the web. Viskanta’s curated selection of content covers a wide range of topics, making it an invaluable resource for investors looking to stay up-to-date on the latest news and trends.

7. The Epicurean Dealmaker
The Epicurean Dealmaker, authored by Mihir Desai, is a must-read blog for investors interested in the intersection of finance and economics. Desai covers topics such as corporate finance, financial regulation, and economic policy, with a focus on providing insights into the complex forces shaping the global economy.

8. The Reformed Broker
Written by Josh Brown, a former Wall Street broker turned financial blogger, The Reformed Broker offers a unique perspective on the world of finance. Brown’s blog covers topics such as market trends, investment strategies, and industry news, all with a focus on providing a fresh take on the latest developments.

9. The Compound Interest
The Compound Interest is a blog written by Joshua Brown and Barry Ritholtz, two of the most respected voices in the financial industry. The blog covers a wide range of topics, including market analysis, investment strategies, and personal finance, all with a focus on helping readers build long-term wealth.

10. The Financial Times Alphaville
The Financial Times Alphaville blog is a must-read for investors looking for in-depth analysis of market trends, economic data, and geopolitical events. The blog’s team of writers and contributors provide expert commentary and insights on the latest developments in the world of finance, making it an invaluable resource for investors.

In conclusion, following these top 10 financial blogs can help you stay informed and make better investment decisions. By learning from industry experts and gaining insights into the latest trends, you can build a solid foundation for long-term financial success. So, take the time to explore these blogs, and start expanding your financial knowledge today.
