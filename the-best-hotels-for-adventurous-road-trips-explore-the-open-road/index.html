The Best Hotels for Adventurous Road Trips: Explore the Open Road

4 min · 642 words

Embarking on an adventurous road trip can be an exhilarating experience, and choosing the right hotel can make it even more memorable. Whether you’re cruising along the scenic Pacific Coast Highway or navigating the rugged terrain of the Rocky Mountains, having a comfortable and convenient place to rest is essential. Here’s a curated list of the best hotels for adventurous road trips, offering not just a place to stay, but an integral part of the journey itself.

1. The Ahwahnee Hotel, Yosemite National Park, California
Nestled in the heart of Yosemite National Park, The Ahwahnee Hotel offers a perfect blend of luxury and wilderness. Surrounded by towering granite cliffs and cascading waterfalls, this historic hotel provides a serene retreat after a day of exploring the park’s breathtaking landscapes. With its rustic charm and modern amenities, The Ahwahnee is an ideal base for adventurers seeking to conquer the park’s iconic trails and natural wonders.

2. Amangiri, Canyon Point, Utah
For road trippers venturing into the otherworldly landscapes of the American Southwest, Amangiri is a sanctuary of contemporary design amidst the rugged desert terrain. This luxury resort seamlessly integrates with the surrounding canyons, offering unparalleled views of the dramatic landscape. After a day of hiking through the otherworldly rock formations of Utah, guests can unwind in the resort’s spa or stargaze from the comfort of their sleek, minimalist suite.

3. The Resort at Paws Up, Greenough, Montana
Set against the backdrop of Montana’s sprawling wilderness, The Resort at Paws Up beckons adventurous travelers with its blend of outdoor adventure and luxurious accommodations. From horseback riding and fly fishing to hot air ballooning and ATV tours, this expansive resort offers an array of activities to satisfy even the most intrepid explorers. After a day of adrenaline-fueled excursions, guests can retire to their well-appointed safari-style tent or luxurious private cabin.

4. The Wickaninnish Inn, Tofino, British Columbia
Perched on the edge of the wild Pacific Ocean, The Wickaninnish Inn is a haven for those seeking an untamed coastal experience. Surrounded by old-growth rainforests and rugged coastline, this award-winning hotel provides an ideal base for road trippers looking to surf the waves, storm watch, or observe the diverse marine life of Vancouver Island. The inn’s oceanfront location and cozy guest rooms offer a front-row seat to nature’s awe-inspiring displays.

5. Under Canvas Zion, Virgin, Utah
For travelers seeking a unique and immersive connection to nature, Under Canvas Zion offers a glamping experience unlike any other. Situated on the doorstep of Zion National Park, this safari-inspired camp provides a luxurious retreat amidst the red rock desert. Guests can unwind in their stylish canvas tent after a day of canyoneering, rock climbing, or exploring the park’s iconic slot canyons.

These distinctive hotels not only provide a comfortable place to recharge but also serve as gateways to some of the most captivating natural wonders in North America. Whether you’re chasing waterfalls in Yosemite, traversing the deserts of Utah, or exploring the untamed beauty of British Columbia, these accommodations are the perfect complement to an adventurous road trip. So pack your bags, hit the open road, and let these hotels be the starting point for your next unforgettable journey into the great outdoors.
