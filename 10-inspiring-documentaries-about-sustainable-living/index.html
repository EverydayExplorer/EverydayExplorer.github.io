10 Inspiring Documentaries About Sustainable Living

4 min · 700 words

In recent years, there has been a growing awareness and interest in sustainable living, as people around the world are becoming more conscious of the impact their actions have on the environment. Documentaries have played a significant role in educating and inspiring viewers to make positive changes in their lives for the betterment of the planet. If you are looking to learn more about sustainable living and be inspired to make a difference, here are 10 documentaries that you should add to your watch list:

1. ‘The True Cost’ (2015) - This eye-opening documentary explores the impact of fast fashion on the environment and the exploitation of workers in the garment industry. It sheds light on the importance of being mindful of where our clothes come from and the true cost of cheap, disposable fashion.

2. ‘Minimalism: A Documentary About the Important Things’ (2015) - Minimalism is not just about decluttering your physical space, but also about living with intention and reducing the impact of consumerism on the planet. This film follows people who have chosen to live with less and find happiness in experiences rather than possessions.

3. ‘Food, Inc.’ (2008) - This documentary exposes the hidden truths behind the food industry and the impact it has on our health, the environment, and the welfare of animals. It encourages viewers to make more informed choices about the food they consume and support sustainable agriculture practices.

4. ‘Plastic Paradise: The Great Pacific Garbage Patch’ (2013) - The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is a massive accumulation of plastic debris in the Pacific Ocean that poses a threat to marine life and ecosystems. This film highlights the urgency of reducing single-use plastic and finding sustainable alternatives to protect our oceans.

5. ‘An Inconvenient Truth’ (2006) - Former Vice President Al Gore presents a compelling case for the reality of climate change and the urgent need for action to address it. This documentary raises awareness about the environmental challenges we face and the importance of reducing our carbon footprint.

6. ‘Chasing Ice’ (2012) - This visually stunning documentary follows photographer James Balog as he captures the dramatic effects of climate change on glaciers around the world. It serves as a powerful reminder of the impact of global warming and the need to protect our planet’s ice.

7. ‘Kiss the Ground’ (2020) - Narrated by Woody Harrelson, this documentary explores regenerative agriculture as a solution to climate change and soil degradation. It showcases innovative farming practices that can help sequester carbon, restore ecosystems, and promote sustainable food production.

8. ‘Before the Flood’ (2016) - Leonardo DiCaprio travels the world to witness the impact of climate change and the efforts being made to combat it. This documentary provides insights into the importance of renewable energy, conservation, and political action in addressing the climate crisis.

9. ‘The Biggest Little Farm’ (2018) - This heartwarming documentary follows a couple as they transform a rundown farm into a biodiverse and sustainable paradise. It showcases the beauty of working in harmony with nature and the rewards of sustainable farming practices.

10. ‘A Plastic Ocean’ (2016) - This documentary exposes the alarming levels of plastic pollution in the world’s oceans and its detrimental effects on marine life. It inspires viewers to rethink their plastic usage and take action to reduce plastic waste for a cleaner and healthier planet.

These documentaries offer valuable insights and inspiration for anyone looking to live a more sustainable lifestyle and make a positive impact on the environment. By learning from the stories and experiences shared in these films, we can all play a part in creating a more sustainable and resilient world for future generations.
